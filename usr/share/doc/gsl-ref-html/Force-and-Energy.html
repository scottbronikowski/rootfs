40.15 Force and Energy

GSL_CONST_MKSA_NEWTON
The SI unit of force, 1 Newton.
GSL_CONST_MKSA_DYNE
The force of 1 Dyne = 10^-5 Newton.
GSL_CONST_MKSA_JOULE
The SI unit of energy, 1 Joule.
GSL_CONST_MKSA_ERG
The energy 1 erg = 10^-7 Joule.
